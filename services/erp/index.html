Enterprise Resource
Planning (ERP) Solutions:

RoseTech Solutions Ltd offers a comprehensive cloud-based Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) solution for businesses of all sizes. Our ERP solution is designed to streamline business processes and increase productivity, efficiency, and profitability. Our team of experts provides customized solutions that cater to the specific requirements of your business.


Our ERP solution is a complete business management system that comprises several integrated modules. These modules include:

Account
Management System

Our Account management system is a comprehensive suite of tools that allows businesses to manage their accounting and financial processes from end to end. It includes tools for managing accounts receivable and payable, budgeting, cash management, fixed assets, and general ledger. Our cloud-based financial management system provides businesses with real-time financial information to help them make informed decisions.

SUPPLY
CHAIN MANAGEMENT

Our supply chain management system allows businesses to manage their entire supply chain, from sourcing and procurement to inventory and warehouse management. It includes tools for forecasting demand, managing inventory levels, and tracking shipments. With our supply chain management system, businesses can optimize their operations to ensure that products are delivered on time and at the lowest possible cost.

HUMAN
RESOURCE MANAGEMENT

Our human resource management system is designed to help businesses manage their workforce more efficiently. It includes tools for recruitment, employee onboarding, time and attendance management, performance evaluation, and employee benefits management. Our HR management system can help businesses reduce administrative costs, improve employee engagement, and increase productivity.

CUSTOMER
RELATIONSHIP MANAGEMENT

Our customer relationship management system allows businesses to manage their customer interactions more effectively. It includes tools for managing leads, tracking customer interactions, and providing customer support. With our CRM system, businesses can improve their customer satisfaction and loyalty, leading to increased revenue and profitability.

PROJECT
MANAGEMENT

Our project management system provides businesses with tools for planning, executing, and monitoring projects. It includes features for task management, resource allocation, budgeting, and project reporting. With our project management system, businesses can increase the efficiency of their project teams, leading to improved project outcomes.

INVENTORY & WAREHOUSE
MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Our inventory management system provides a complete view of your inventory, including stock levels, movement, and location. It helps businesses to make informed decisions by providing real-time inventory data, automated reordering, and detailed reports. It includes features such as bill of materials (BOM) management, work order management, and production scheduling. Our system allows businesses to track the status of each production order and schedule resources efficiently, which leads to improved productivity and reduced waste.

Our ERP solution has several
advantages, including:

Improved efficiency: Our ERP solution streamlines business processes, reducing the need for manual work, which increases productivity and efficiency.

Real-time data: Our ERP solution provides real-time data, allowing businesses to make informed decisions quickly.

Enhanced customer service: Our ERP solution includes a CRM module that improves customer service by managing customer data and interactions.

Cost savings: Our ERP solution eliminates the need for multiple systems, reducing maintenance costs and increasing profitability.
